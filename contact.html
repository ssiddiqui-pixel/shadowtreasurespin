Contact the team

We maintain an always-on inbox because timely human support is part of our promise.

Concierge inbox

Email concierge@shadowtreasurespin.com or use the form below to request curated playlists, accessibility adjustments, or press material. We respond within one business day because we know inspiration often strikes late at night. You will receive a confirmation message with a tracking number so you can monitor progress, and follow-up notes include transparent timestamps.

Studio partnerships

Indie teams can submit upcoming releases for consideration. Share a playable build, art bible, accessibility checklist, and any cultural references we should honor. We schedule private playtests, provide actionable notes, and only feature a title if it keeps monetization turned off by default. Expect a collaborative memo that documents every next step.

Community care

We run quarterly listening sessions to gather feedback from neurodiverse players, parents, and caregivers. Their insights shape our wellness reminders, color palettes, and optional timer nudges. Participants receive meeting summaries, and we publicly log which ideas shipped so the community can see its impact.
